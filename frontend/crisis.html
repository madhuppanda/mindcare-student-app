🌿 Coping Strategies for Right Now

🌬️
Box Breathing
Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat 4 times to calm your nervous system.
🖐️
5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Brings you to the present moment.
💧
Splash Cold Water
Splash cold water on your face or hold ice — it activates the dive reflex and slows your heart rate quickly.
🤝
Reach Out
Call or text someone you trust. You don't have to explain everything — just say "I need someone to talk to."
🚶
Move Your Body
A short walk, even around the room, can shift your emotional state. Movement is medicine for the mind.
✍️
Write It Out
Write down exactly what you're feeling without judgment. Externalising thoughts reduces their intensity.
